How I caused a million dollar recall and lived to tell the tale

A series of small errors over two years combine to cause the recall of a medical device. Hilarity ensues.

Imagine your name being on the commit that causes a medical device to fail. Imagine that failure costs a about a million dollars to fix. This is the story of that happening to me: how I got into that mess; what led me there, what tripped me up, and how we dealt with it once it happened.
